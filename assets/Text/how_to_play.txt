Objective:
    Survive 10 Waves

Controls:
    Movement:                                          WASD/Arrow keys
    Spray / Start Wave / Select Upgrade:        Space
    Plant Berry / Eat Berry / Select Upgrade:    E
    Toggle Upgrade Shop:                             Q

Tips:
    Enemy waves are exhausting; eat a berry when low on health or spray
    Squirrels go NUTS for berries; kill them to retrieve stolen berries
    Skunks love snacking; upgrade your skunk between waves
